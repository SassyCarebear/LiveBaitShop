A little about us

Howard's store provides locals and customers with many of the basic necessities as well as the needs for traveling, camping and outdoor activities known to our little township. We are known for our subs and soft serve ice cream cones to many of our local and returning customers. The store has been around for over a hundred years providing items and supplies to all the people of PCV whether they are locals, have cabins or family homes here or just visiting, and/or passing through. We intend to keep it this way for many years to come.

Thank you for visiting with us!
